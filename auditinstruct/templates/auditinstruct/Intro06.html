At the end of each day/round, you will learn whether your sample led to the correct decision. You will get a report that tells you whether the decision to agree or disagree (as determined by your sample results), is either a:.

1. Correct agreement. This occurs when your sample results lead you to accept an accurate shipment.
2. Wrong agreement. This occurs when your sample results lead you to accept an incorrect shipment.
3. Correct disagreement. This occurs when your sample results lead you to reject an incorrect shipment.
4. Wrong disagreement. This occurs when your sample results lead you to reject an accurate shipment.
