Sampling a portion of the box's goods is your only way of determining whether you will agree or disagree with the bill of lading. For example, if your sample results suggest that the reported count is a correct description of the box, you will automatically agree with the bill of lading and accept the shipment. If, on the other hand, your sample results suggest that the reported count is incorrect, then you will automatically disagree with the bill of lading and reject the shipment. If you decide to sample 0 items from the box, you will automatically agree with the bill of lading and accept the shipment.

Your supervisor has advised only to reject a box if the distribution of goods from your sample is more than 10 of one item different than the bill of lading. Other employees of the Cafe will verify whether or not you made the right decision as the goods are sold throughout the day.
