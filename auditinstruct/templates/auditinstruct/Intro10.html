At the end of the last round, you will be paid $10 for finishing plus extra cash depending on your cumulative earnings. The amount of extra cash you will receive (above the $10 minimum) will be based on your RELATIVE performance across all participants in your session. The average payment to participants will be $15.50 total, with the best performers receiving as much as $20 total, and the worst performers receiving a minimum of $11.

Again, your payment will depend on your performance RELATIVE to other participants in this session. During the course of the study, you may experience an overall net loss in experimental dollars. A negative experiment balance does NOT mean you are losing “real” money. Because your extra cash payment depends on your performance relative to all other participants, you will not be paid until all participants in this session complete the study.

To help you maintain a good pace during the study, there is a countdown timer on each screen. Please try to submit your responses each round before the countdown timer reaches "0."
