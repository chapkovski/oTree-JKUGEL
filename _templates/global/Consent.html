Thank you for agreeing to participate in this research study! The research is being conducted by Dr. Jason (Xi) Kuang and Jonathan Kugel (both at Georgia Tech). At a minimum, you will be guaranteed the pro-rated, Prolific minimum of $6.50/hour. Additional earnings may be possible based on your actions in the experiment. The study is expected to take 20-30 minutes to complete.

Approximately 180 participants will complete this study in groups of three or six. Participants in this study must be > 18 years old, located in the United States, and duly registered and in good standing with the Prolific Platform. Your will confirm your eligibility to participate on the next page. If you do not qualify, you will not be eligible for payment or participation.

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary. You have the right to change your mind and leave the study at any time without giving any reason and without penalty. You do not waive any of your legal rights by consenting to participate. You will be awarded whatever compensation you have thus far earned at the time of your study termination.

This study is being conducted in a double-blind setting: participants are not known to each other or to the experimenters. Your actions and decisions in this study are completely anonymous. Additionally, the researchers pledge that this study does not involve or utilize deception of any kind.

No personally identifying information about you will be provided to the experimenters at any time. The results of this research project will be reported only in aggregate form. The data that are collected about you will be kept private to the extent required by law. Study records will be kept confidential to the extent required by law. To make sure that this research is being carried out in the proper way, the Georgia Institute of Technology IRB may review study records. The Office of Human Research Protections may also look at study records.

You are not likely to benefit in any way from joining this study. We hope to better understand judgments made in the accounting professions. There are no costs to you, other than your time, for being in this study. There are no anticipated risks to your participation in this study other than those ordinarily encountered in day-to-day life.

Additional questions, complaints, or reports of injury resulting from participation in this experiment should be made to Dr. Jason (Xi) Kuang at Jason.Kuang@Scheller.gatech.edu, or Ms. Melanie Clark, Georgia Institute of Technology, Office of Research Integrity Assurance, at (404) 894-6942.

Again, thank you for your participation and providing your valuable time!
